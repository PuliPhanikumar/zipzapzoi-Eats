Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026 | Compliant with DPDP Act 2023

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Hostel Owner Data: Name, email, mobile number, government ID (Aadhaar/PAN), business address.
  • Hosteler Data: Name, Aadhaar number, parent/guardian details, emergency contacts, blood group, college/company affiliation.
  • Financial Data: Billing records, payment history, GST details.
  • Usage Data: Login activity, feature usage patterns, device and browser information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the Zoi Hostel POS service.
  • To process payments and generate GST invoices.
  • To verify identity through Aadhaar-based KYC.
  • To provide AI-driven insights and predictive analytics.
  • To communicate service updates and support.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian law.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on encrypted servers hosted in India. We use 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Access to sensitive data (Aadhaar, financial records) is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Payment processors for transaction processing.
  • Government authorities when required by law.
  • Cloud service providers for hosting (within India only).

5. Your Rights (Under DPDP Act 2023)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Contact our Data Protection Officer.
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate a person to exercise rights in case of death or incapacity.

6. Aadhaar Data Handling

Aadhaar numbers are collected solely for KYC compliance. They are stored in encrypted format and are never shared with third parties except as mandated by UIDAI regulations.

7. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Analytics cookies (anonymized) help us improve the service. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

8. Data Retention

Active account data is retained throughout the subscription. After account termination, data is retained for 90 days for export, then permanently deleted. Financial records are retained for 7 years as per Indian tax law.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under 18. If a hosteler is a minor, parental/guardian consent is required and recorded during admission.
