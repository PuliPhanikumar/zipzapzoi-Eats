Our Origin

The Story of ZipZapZoi Eats

It started with a simple frustration: choosing what to eat was too complicated, and the delivery experience was too unpredictable. In 2023, a team of food enthusiasts and AI engineers came together to solve this problem.

We envisioned a platform that didn't just list restaurants, but actively understood its users. A platform where the interface felt alive, intuitive, and remarkably fast.

Today, ZipZapZoi Eats serves millions of customized meals daily across India. By bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and local culinary arts, we are redefining convenience and elevating the everyday dining experience.
