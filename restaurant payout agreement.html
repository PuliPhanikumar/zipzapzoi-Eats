Partner Agreement

1. Definitions. "Platform" refers to ZipZapZoi Eats. "Partner" refers to the restaurant entity.

2. Commission & Fees. The Partner agrees to pay a commission of 22% on the gross order value excluding taxes. ZipZapZoi reserves the right to adjust these rates with a 30-day notice period.

3. Payout Schedule. Net earnings will be transferred via NEFT/RTGS to the registered bank account every Wednesday for the sales cycle ending the previous Sunday.

4. Food Safety. The Partner warrants that all food prepared complies with FSSAI regulations and hygiene standards.

5. Intellectual Property. Partner grants ZipZapZoi a non-exclusive license to use its name, logo, and menu content for marketing purposes on the Platform.

6. Termination. Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice.
